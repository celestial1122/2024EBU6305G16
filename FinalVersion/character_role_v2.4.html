Emma Bloom (Brought to life by: Ella Purnell)

What's Her Spark? Emma's not just the girl next door; she's the girl who can start a bonfire in the rain. Call her the matchless match girl, with a touch that's both warm-hearted and, well, scorcher-hot when villains lurk.

A Flame in the Game: Whether she's lighting up the dark corners of the children's home or sparking hope when the going gets tough, Emma's the bright spot on a cloudy day. She's the kind of friend who'll walk through fire for you—literally—and come out without a scorch mark

Get set to catch some sparks with Emma as she ignites a path to adventure that's as fiery as her spirit. For a flicker of fun and a blaze of bravery, Ella Purnell's Emma is the flame that never flickers out. Ready, set, glow!
