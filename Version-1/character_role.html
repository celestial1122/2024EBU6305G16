Just your average American teen until life went “Hold my beer.” Post-grandpa's spooky demise, Jacob's life gets a fantasy reboot. Now he sees monsters that are RSVP'ed to the “Invisible to Normals” party. Protector of the peculiar? You bet.

Miss Peregrine (Eva Green)

Bird woman, time-loop manager, protector of the peculiar. Miss Peregrine's got the whole package. Her daily routine includes keeping kids safe and bending time -- casual stuff.

What's Her Deal? Meet Olivia, a girl who never needs a ladder. Why? She's got the power of levitation, making gravity her plaything. School high jump champ without even trying.

Emma, wielding fire like it's her personal plaything, is the squad's unofficial captain. Bravery? Check. Loyalty? Double check.
