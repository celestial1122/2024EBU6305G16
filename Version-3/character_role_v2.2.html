Miss Peregrine (Portrayed by: Eva Green)

What's Her Quirk? Say hello to Miss Peregrine, the time-twisting, bird-shifting guardian of all things whimsical. She's not just the headmistress of a home for gifted youngsters; she's a superhero in a skirt, turning back the clock—literally—on the daily.

Tick-Tock, Beat the Clock: With a stopwatch for a heart and a hawk's eye for trouble, Miss Peregrine keeps her peculiar brood safe within a 24-hour time loop. Tea at precisely four, monsters at the door? Not on her watch!

Buckle up for a flight of fancy with Miss Peregrine as she swoops in to save the day before you can say “cuckoo.” Who needs a cape when you have a corset that defies time itself? Watch as Eva Green brings this fierce, feathered phenom to life—because with Miss Peregrine, every day is an encore!
