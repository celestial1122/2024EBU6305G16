Jacob Portman (Played by: Asa Butterfield)

What's His Superpower? Meet Jacob: just a regular teen who traded in video games for monster-slaying adventures. With the gift of seeing the unseen, he's the kid who's putting the "peculiar" in Miss Peregrine's Home.

Monster Mash and Daring Dash: Jacob doesn't just chase the bus; he chases away creatures that go bump in the night. When he's not busy being the resident hero, he's probably unraveling the coolest family mysteries. Who needs ancient relics when your grandpa leaves behind a time-looping, monster-packed legacy?
